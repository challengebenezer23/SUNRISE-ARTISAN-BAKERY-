About Us

Our Story

SUNRISE ARTISAN BAKERY was founded in 2018, Sunrise Artisan Bakery was born from a passion for wholesome, handcrafted bread and pastries made with care, quality ingredients, and a commitment to community. From our humble beginnings, we set out to create more than just baked goods. we wanted to share the warmth, tradition, and joy that fresh bread brings to every table. Our mission is to craft nutritious, delicious, and affordable baked products that celebrate both tradition and innovation. We are dedicated to using the finest ingredients, supporting local suppliers where possible, and ensuring every loaf, roll, and pastry reflects the love and artistry of true baking. At Sunrise Artisan Bakery, we believe in more than baking. We believe in nourishing our community, one loaf at a time.
